Hello Lali,

That's a fairly difficult thing to do. First, a standard staircase is probably just too high for Nao, I think you can't really walk up the stair, but you'll have to "climb" it up.

Second, even if you manage to find a good "animation" for doing this, the perception capabilities you need to actually do it in real situations (not perfectly positioned ...) are quite out of reach of Nao. You'd need some better perception, like a depth sensor (asus xtion which you can use on Nao), or laser range finder.

Have a look at what these guys are doing, they have a custom Nao with Hokuyo laser range finder on its head, and I think the staircase is not human-sized, but reduced for Nao. Last but not least, they're quite good at robotics:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h26nefX0lU8
